Our Story

From First Admiral to Future Oceans

Named after Kara Mursel Alp, the first Ottoman Admiral, we carry forward a 700-year legacy of maritime leadership.

Why Mursel?

A Legacy of Maritime Vision

In the early 14th century, Kara Mursel Alp established the foundations of Ottoman naval power. His strategic vision transformed a regional force into a Mediterranean powerhouse.

At Mursel Ventures, we believe that same strategic thinking is needed today -- not for conquest, but for sustainable ocean innovation. We exist to connect visionary entrepreneurs with the capital, expertise, and networks they need to turn ocean challenges into global opportunities.
