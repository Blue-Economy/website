Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Mursel Ventures through our website, forms, or communications, we may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, phone number)
  • Professional information (company, role, LinkedIn profile)
  • Application data submitted through our forms
  • Website usage data through cookies and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process applications to our programs (Venture Studio, Accelerator, Consulting, Fund)
  • Match startups with relevant mentors and partners
  • Send newsletters and updates (with your consent)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data, including encryption, access controls, and regular security assessments.

4. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR and KVKK), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request data portability

5. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies for functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including Google Analytics, social media platforms, and email providers. These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this policy. Application data is retained for up to 24 months after the last interaction.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at info@murselventures.com.
